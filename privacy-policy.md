# Privacy Policy for One Badge

Effective Date: 28-01-2026

One Badge respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles user information.

## Information Collection
One Badge does not require users to create an account and does not collect personally identifiable information such as name, email address, phone number, or location.

## Data Usage
The app fetches football-related data such as team details, match information, and league standings from public sports APIs. This data is used only to display football information within the app.

## Local Storage
One Badge stores selected team preferences locally on the user’s device using local storage mechanisms. This data never leaves the device and is not shared with any third party.

## Third-Party Services
The app may use third-party sports data providers such as TheSportsDB to retrieve football information. No personal user data is shared with these services.

## Internet Access
An internet connection is required to fetch football data. Cached data may be displayed when offline.

## Children’s Privacy
One Badge is suitable for all age groups (3+). The app does not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy
This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
